Tip: Minimally viable product

When building a product, IMO:

Don‚Äôt: Try to do everything completely and perfectly from the beginning. This leads to perfectionist thinking, which leads to procrastination and ‚Äúanalysis paralysis‚Äù.

Do: Start by finishing a minimially viable product FAST:

Only you‚Äôre done your MVP, iterate and improve by adding complexity.

In other words:

  • Done is better than perfect
  • Don‚Äôt let the perfect be the enemy of the good
  • Think big, start small
